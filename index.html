Is your programming language unreasonable?

Scott writes about why predictability of programming languages matters.

"Reasoning about the code" means that you can draw conclusions using only the information that you have right in front of you, rather than having to delve into other parts of the codebase. In other words, you can predict the behavior of some code just by looking at it.
