Workshops and training sessions

We believe strongly in the benefits of using F#, but we also understand the issues and challenges around adding F# into your software development process. Thats's why we offer training, support and other services to make your use of F# as painless as possible.

If you would like to arrange a private, personalized workshop, please contact us directly.

General information

The following infomation applies to all workshops.

Prerequisites:

  • No prior F# experience needed.
  • No prior experience needed with the theme of the workshop (e.g. Machine Learning).
  • Previous programming experience is required.
  • Experience with .NET and C# is desirable.

Equipment needed:

  • Laptop with F# installed (see fsharp.org for installation instructions)
