Welcome to the second annual F# survey!

As the F# community continues to grow, we at fsharpWorks think that it is helpful to all of us to get hard data on how we use F#, what is going well, and what needs more work. We hope that the answers to these questions will provide actionable data that can used by the community to improve F#.

Plus, it's a bit of fun! Who doesn't love looking at surveys?

This is NOT an "official" survey sponsored by the F# Software Foundation or Microsoft. It is sponsored by us at fsharpWorks, as a fun way to share information about the F# community.

The survey

We want a wide spectrum of people to participate, not just experts! So, even if you are just curious about F#, we would like to know what you think.

The results

The survey will be open for one week, from April 12th 2016, to midnight April 19th 2016. The results will be published on this site and social media shortly thereafter.
