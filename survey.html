Welcome to the first annual F# survey!

As the the F# community grows, we at fsharpWorks think that it will be helpful to all of us to get hard data on how we use F#, what is going well, and what needs more work. We hope that the answers to these questions will provide actionable data that can used by the community to improve F#.

Also, if we repeat this survey regularly, we hope to monitor the growth and changes in the F# community over time.

Finally, it's a bit of fun! Who doesn't love looking at surveys?

This is NOT an "official" survey sponsored by the F# Software Foundation or Microsoft. It is sponsored by us at fsharpWorks, as a fun way to share information about the F# community.

The survey

The survey was open for one week, from April 12th 2015, to midnight April 19th 2015, and has now closed.

If you filled it in, thanks for participating, otherwise, hope to get your input next time!
